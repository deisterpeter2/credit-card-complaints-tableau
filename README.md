# Credit Card Complaint Dashboard (Tableau)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes **consumer credit card complaints** using **Tableau** to identify complaint trends, response performance, and the most frequently reported customer issues.

The interactive dashboard enables business users to explore complaint volume over time, monitor customer service performance, and identify areas requiring operational improvement.

## Business Problem

Financial institutions receive thousands of customer complaints each year. Understanding complaint trends, response performance, and recurring customer issues helps organizations improve customer satisfaction, prioritize operational improvements, and monitor service quality.

This project answers the following business questions:

- How has complaint volume changed over time?
- Which complaint categories occur most frequently?
- How effectively are customer complaints being resolved?
- Which areas require the greatest operational attention?

---

## Dataset

**Source:** Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)

The dataset contains consumer complaints related to credit card products, including:

- Date Received
- Complaint Category
- Company Response
- Timely Response Indicator
- Complaint Status

## Project Objectives

### 1. Analyze Complaint Trends

Track complaint volume over time to identify changes in customer complaint activity.

**Deliverables**

- Complaint trend line chart

- Trend analysis

### 2. Identify Common Complaint Categories

Determine which complaint types are reported most frequently.

**Deliverables**

Design an easy-to-use dashboard that enables business users to quickly explore complaint trends and performance metrics.

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Summary

The dashboard includes key performance indicators to provide an at-a-glance view of customer complaint performance.

- Total Complaints
- Timely Responses
- Timely Response Rate (%)
- In-Progress Complaints
- In-Progress Complaint Rate (%)

---

### Complaint Trend

A line chart displays complaint volume over time.

Users can filter the dashboard using an interactive date range.

---

### Complaint Heat Map

A heat map highlights complaint distribution, allowing users to quickly identify areas with the highest complaint volume.

---

### Top 10 Complaint Categories

A ranked horizontal bar chart displays the complaint categories with the highest number of customer complaints.
